Watch Out

Why Some Flats Take Months to Sell

Six problems behind almost every stale HDB listing. My process is built to avoid each one.

💰
Priced on last year's numbers
The market moves monthly. Price on old benchmarks and the flat sits, then chases the market down.
📸
Weak presentation
Dark phone photos against listings with professional shots. Buyers scroll past before they see the flat's strengths.
🚪
Unqualified viewings
Viewers with no HFE letter or financing burn your weekends. They were never able to buy.
📄
OTP technicalities
Option monies and timelines have strict rules. Getting them wrong is how done deals fall apart.
⏱️
Timeline collisions
The sale completes before your next home is ready. Proper sequencing keeps you from renting in between.
📨
Agents who just forward messages
Passing offers back and forth is not negotiation. If your agent doesn't know what your block closes at, the buyer's agent does.

The Process

How I Sell Your Flat

Six steps, start to keys. You'll know exactly where your sale stands at every moment.

Free valuation & strategy call
We start with the number. I run your block's recent transactions, walk the unit, and give you a realistic range plus a pricing strategy: list price and negotiation floor. Try the instant estimate first if you're curious.
Eligibility & timeline check
Before anything goes live, we make sure your MOP is satisfied and go through your sale proceeds (CPF refund, loan redemption, cash). If you're buying your next home, we work out both transactions and walk through the timeline together so you transition smoothly.
Preparation & marketing
Decluttering advice, professional photos, and a listing written to sell. Your flat goes up on the major portals and in front of my own buyer network. Many of my deals match before the listing is even public.
Viewings & negotiation
I conduct every viewing personally, qualify buyers (HFE letter, financing) before they waste your weekends, and negotiate from a position of knowledge, because I know what every comparable unit in your area actually closed at.
Option to Purchase
When we accept an offer, I handle all the OTP paperwork, facilitate the transfer of the option fee, and manage both the seller's and buyer's timelines. This is where many DIY sellers and buyers run into conflict.
Completion at HDB
From OTP acceptance, HDB's resale process takes roughly 8 weeks to completion. I track every milestone (resale application, HDB approval, final inspection, key handover) and keep you updated at each one.

Common Questions

Selling Your HDB, Answered

When can I sell my HDB flat?
Once you've completed your Minimum Occupation Period: 5 years for most flats, counted from when you collected keys. Not sure of your exact date? WhatsApp me your block and I'll verify it.
How long does it take to sell an HDB flat?
Finding the buyer: most of my listings take 2–4 weeks. See the track record, which includes a flat sold in one viewing. After OTP, HDB's completion process adds roughly 8 weeks. So budget about 3 months end to end.
How much is my flat worth right now?
Start with the free instant valuation. It uses the last 12 months of official data.gov.sg transactions for your town and flat type. Then I'll give you a precise number adjusted for your exact unit, free.
What does it cost to sell through you?
Commission is agreed upfront, transparently, before you sign anything. Nothing is payable until your sale actually completes. Ask me directly and I'll give you a straight answer in the first conversation.
Can I sell my flat and buy the next one at the same time?
Yes, and this is where good planning pays off most. With the right sequencing, and contra where it fits, you move straight from one home to the next without renting in between. Tell me your situation and I'll map the timeline.
