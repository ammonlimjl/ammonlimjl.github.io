Buy & Sell HDB in Tampines

Singapore's eastern regional centre. Live prices and the demand behind them.

Tampines is one of my three core specialties, and the easiest town in Singapore to explain to a buyer: it's a regional centre with jobs, three malls, a town hub with everything from a hawker centre to an Olympic-size pool, plus a mature resale market to match. Tampines regularly posts some of the highest HDB transaction volumes in the country, which makes pricing both easier (plenty of comparable sales) and less forgiving (buyers can see exactly what your neighbour sold for).

Local Knowledge

6 Things That Move Tampines Prices

A regional centre that actually works
Tampines was Singapore's first regional centre, and the jobs are real: banks' back offices, IKEA, Changi Business Park ten minutes away. Working near home is a genuine reason buyers pay Tampines prices.
Three malls and Our Tampines Hub
Tampines Mall, Century Square and Tampines 1 sit side by side at the MRT, and Our Tampines Hub packs a stadium, library, hawker centre and pools into the country's biggest integrated community hub. Few towns can match this amenity density.
Triple rail, with more coming
The East-West Line and Downtown Line already serve Tampines, Tampines East and Tampines West. The Cross Island Line will add Tampines North later this decade, and blocks near the future station are worth watching.
Mature estate, liquid market
Tampines posts some of the highest resale volumes in Singapore year after year. High liquidity means fair pricing sells fast, and overpricing is noticed quickly because buyers have plenty of comparables.
Lease age varies street by street
Unlike Punggol, Tampines spans flats from the 1980s to recent BTOs. Two 4-room flats a street apart can differ by 20 years of lease and six figures in price. Block-level comparison matters more here than almost anywhere.
New supply is coming in Tampines North
New BTO clusters in Tampines North will feed future resale supply and bring fresh amenities. Sellers in surrounding areas should price with that pipeline in mind; buyers get a rare 'new Tampines' option.

Common Questions

Tampines HDB Questions, Answered

How much does a 4-room HDB in Tampines cost?
The live table above has the current median and range, refreshed monthly from data.gov.sg. Lease age drives big differences here: a 1980s flat and a 2010s flat a street apart price very differently. Get a block-specific figure via the free valuation tool.
Is Tampines a good town to buy an HDB flat in?
For amenities and resale liquidity, it's hard to beat. The one thing to check carefully is remaining lease, since Tampines spans four decades of construction. I can tell you which blocks give the best lease-to-price balance for your budget.
How fast do Tampines HDB flats sell?
Fast, if priced right. This is a high-volume market and well-priced flats clear in 2–4 weeks. Buyers here can see every comparable sale, so overpricing gets noticed quickly. It pays to launch at the right number from day one.
What will the Cross Island Line do to Tampines prices?
The new Tampines North station will help the blocks currently furthest from rail. If you own near the future station, the market typically starts pricing in confirmed lines ahead of opening, which is worth factoring into your sell-now-or-wait decision. Ask me for a block-level read.
