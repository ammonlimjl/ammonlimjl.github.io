Buy & Sell HDB in Sengkang

Where I started out. Live prices and what actually moves them in Sengkang.

Sengkang is where I started, and it remains one of Singapore's most reliable family towns. It sits in a sweet spot: younger and better-priced than mature central estates, but more established than Punggol next door, with schools, malls and a major hospital already in place. That balance shows up in the data: steady transaction volumes and resilient prices across 4-room and 5-room flats.

Here's the live market picture and the local forces I weigh when pricing a Sengkang flat.

Local Knowledge

6 Things That Move Sengkang Prices

Built for families
Dense childcare, sought-after schools and Riverside Park make it a top pick for young families.
Sengkang General Hospital brings steady demand
The hospital campus brings thousands of healthcare workers, supporting resale and rental demand.
Compass One and the LRT loops
Compass One sits above the MRT interchange, with two LRT loops putting most blocks a short ride from town.
Sengkang Grand at Buangkok
This integrated development added a mall, hawker centre and childcare, lifting nearby blocks.
More mature than Punggol, younger than Hougang
Settled estates and amenities, but leases that are still long: a sweet spot for upgraders.
Rivervale to Anchorvale: price by district
Each district prices differently by LRT access and school catchment, so I price by block, not town average.

Common Questions

Sengkang HDB Questions, Answered

How much does a 4-room HDB in Sengkang cost?
The live table above shows the current median and range from official data.gov.sg transactions, refreshed monthly. District matters: Rivervale, Compassvale, Anchorvale and Fernvale each price differently. Get a block-specific number with the free valuation tool.
Is Sengkang a good place for families to buy an HDB?
It's one of the most dependable family choices in Singapore. Schools, hospital, malls and parks are already in place, and leases are still long. The volume of transactions in the live table above tells you how liquid this market is: when you eventually sell, there will be buyers.
How is the rental market for Sengkang HDB flats?
Strong. Hospital staff and families waiting out BTO construction keep both whole-flat and room rentals moving. If you're weighing renting out versus selling, WhatsApp me and I'll run both numbers for your block.
Can I sell my Sengkang flat and upgrade in the same move?
Yes, and it's one of the most common moves I handle. With the right timeline (and contra where it fits), you avoid paying for two homes at once or scrambling for temporary housing. The earlier you plan the sequence, the more options you have.
