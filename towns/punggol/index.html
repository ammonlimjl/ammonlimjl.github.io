Buy & Sell HDB in Punggol

My home turf. Live resale prices, what moves them, and deals I've closed here.

Above Punggol Waterway, the heart of my HDB practice.

Punggol is Singapore's youngest HDB town, and the one I know best. I grew up in the North-East, most of my closed transactions are here, and I track its prices block by block. Because almost every flat in Punggol was built after 2000, buyers get some of the longest remaining leases in the resale market, and sellers face a market unlike anywhere else: thousands of nearly-identical flats reaching their 5-year MOP at the same time. That makes pricing strategy matter more in Punggol than in any mature estate.

Local Knowledge

7 Things That Move Punggol Prices

Singapore's longest leases
Punggol's oldest blocks date to the early 2000s and most are far newer, so resale buyers routinely get 85–95 years of remaining lease, a key reason young families upgrade here instead of into older estates.
Punggol Digital District is arriving
JTC's Digital District beside Punggol Coast MRT is progressively opening, bringing the Singapore Institute of Technology campus and a planned tech-business hub measured in tens of thousands of jobs. That demand did not exist when most owners bought.
Punggol Coast MRT opened
The North East Line extension to Punggol Coast (NE17) opened in December 2024, putting the Digital District and Northshore blocks on the rail map and lifting the appeal of flats in walking distance.
The MOP wave cuts both ways
Punggol has one of the biggest waves of flats crossing their Minimum Occupation Period in Singapore. For buyers it means choice; for sellers it means competing against neighbours with near-identical units, so presentation and pricing make or break the sale.
Waterway living draws buyers
My Waterway @ Punggol, Coney Island and the Punggol Waterway Park give the town a lifestyle that consistently pulls in young families. Waterway-facing and park-edge blocks sell at a visible premium.
Northshore's smart-home features
Northshore was HDB's first smart-enabled district, with pneumatic waste systems, smart lighting and EV-ready parking. These newer-generation features are becoming differentiators as the first Northshore flats hit the resale market.
LRT coverage decides walkability
The two Punggol LRT loops mean a flat's value is shaped by which station it sits beside more than raw distance to the MRT. I price by station, not by town average.

For a number specific to your block, use the free valuation tool.
Is Punggol a good place to buy an HDB flat?
If you value lease length and new infrastructure, yes. The current MOP supply wave also gives buyers real negotiating room. Several of my recent Punggol purchases closed at zero COV. The right block matters though: LRT station, waterway frontage and Digital District proximity all move the price.
How long does it take to sell an HDB flat in Punggol?
Priced correctly against the MOP-wave competition, 2–4 weeks is realistic. My Punggol record includes a flat sold in one viewing. Price against last year's benchmarks instead of this month's, though, and a listing can sit for months.
Should I sell my Punggol flat now or wait?
Depends on your block's cohort: if many neighbouring units cross MOP shortly after yours, going to market early usually beats competing with them six months later. WhatsApp me your block and I'll give you a free read on your street's supply timeline.
