13,480 HDB Flats Hit MOP in 2026 — Nearly Double Last Year's Wave
A record 13,480 HDB flats are reaching their Minimum Occupation Period this year, up from 6,970 in 2025. Punggol leads with 3,222 units, followed by Queenstown (2,409) and Tampines (2,133). Analysts forecast 26,000–27,000 resale transactions in 2026 with price growth of 2–5%.

Serenity Park Launches S$505M En Bloc — Freehold OCR Gem
Freehold Serenity Park (off Yio Chu Kang Road) launched for collective sale at S$505M, closing March 26. Experts predict more en bloc activity — especially from freehold suburban and city-fringe developments looking to unlock value.
